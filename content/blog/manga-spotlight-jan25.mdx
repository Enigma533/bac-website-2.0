# **The Summit of the Gods**

<div align="center" style = {{marginTop: '-15px', marginBottom: '-15px'}}>
    <img src="/images/blog/manga-spotlight-jan25/summit-1.webp"/>
</div>

_The Summit of the Gods_ is a masterful manga adaptation of Baku Yumemakura's novel, illustrated by Jiro Taniguchi. This five-volume series blends the thrill of mountaineering with a profound exploration of human ambition and obsession. The story follows Fukamachi Makoto, a photojournalist, who becomes entangled in a mystery involving George Mallory's lost camera, potentially holding answers to whether Mallory was the first to summit Everest. This leads Fukamachi to Habu J√¥ji, an enigmatic climber, whose personal journey is equally captivating and tragic.

<div align="center" style = {{marginTop: '-15px', marginBottom: '-15px'}}>
    <img src="/images/blog/manga-spotlight-jan25/summit-2.webp"/>
</div>

Taniguchi's artwork is breathtaking, capturing the grandeur of the Himalayas as well as the glimpses of lifestyle in the valleys of it with meticulous detail. The characters are deeply fleshed out, their motivations and inner struggles are masterfully captured. Themes of pursuing the meaning, power of human spirit against nature's adversity, and the allure of unattainable make it resonate beyond its mountaineering focus.

<div align="center" style = {{marginTop: '-15px', marginBottom: '-15px'}}>
    <img src="/images/blog/manga-spotlight-jan25/summit-3.webp"/>
</div>

It's a gripping read for the fans of adventure, drama, and philosophical storytelling, offering both edge-of-your-seat moments and profound introspection. If you're a fan of works like _The Climber (Kokou no Hito)_ this manga is for you.

# **Toilet-bound Hanako-kun**

<div align="center" style = {{marginTop: '-15px', marginBottom: '-15px'}}>
    <img src="/images/blog/manga-spotlight-jan25/hanako-1.webp"/>
</div>

_Toilet-bound Hanako-kun_ is an absolutely enchanting manga that will pull you into a world of
mystery, magic, and heartwarming moments. The story follows Nene Yashiro, a high school student
who is determined to find love no matter what. Her curiosity leads her to the "Seven Wonders" of
her school, where she summons the infamous Hanako-kun, a supernatural being said to grant
wishes. But when Nene meets him, she discovers that Hanako-kun is nothing like the scary legend
she expected. He's playful, mischievous, and a bit of a troublemaker who has a hidden kindness that
makes him irresistible.

<div align="center" style = {{marginTop: '-15px', marginBottom: '-15px'}}>

</div>

As Nene‚Äôs wish for love turns into an unexpected adventure, she finds herself drawn into the
mysterious world of spirits and school secrets. Along the way, she forms a deep bond with Hanako-
kun, uncovering the truth behind the school‚Äôs supernatural happenings. Their relationship grows in
such a heartfelt and dynamic way, making it impossible not to root for them.

The art is so soft and fluffy, with expressive characters and beautifully detailed scenes that create a
dreamy, otherworldly atmosphere. Every panel feels alive with energy, and the emotions come
through so strongly, making the experience of reading this manga truly immersive. With each
chapter, you'll find yourself more and more captivated, wanting to know what happens next. This
manga is the perfect blend of sweetness, mystery, and a little bit of eerie charm, one of my favourite
manga of all time.
